Unicode: Greek Alphabet α β γ

By Xah Lee. Date: 2016-08-25. Last updated: 2017-01-05.

Here's a complete list of Unicode Greek alphabet characters.

They are styled characters, for example, For example, this 𝜶 is MATHEMATICAL
BOLD ITALIC SMALL ALPHA (U+1D736). It is not a specific font.

They are designed to be used together with several styled English letters used
in math, such as ℝ ℜ ℑ ⅇ ⅈ ⅉ

ς FINAL SIGMA is the form of the Greek letter sigma σ but used as the last
letter of a word. Final sigma is not used in modern Greek.

digamma Ϝ 𝟊 ϝ 𝟋. Digamma is an archaic letter of the Greek alphabet.

Math Variants

MICRO SIGN µ (math variant of Greek letter μ)

Following are character whose Unicode name ends in “symbol”, as opposed to
having “letter” in the name. They are Greek letter variants designed to be used
in Math.
