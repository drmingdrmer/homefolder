What Characters Are Included

The character here must be a matching bracket pair with respect to their
semantics, as indicated in the char's Unicode name.

values of Open and Close are included. See: http://www.unicode.org/Public/
UNIDATA/BidiBrackets.txt (local copy BidiBrackets.txt)
